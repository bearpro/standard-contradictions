### How the model captures the hypothesis

* **Definition rule** (`definition_confidential_information`) allows any of the
  categories – scientific, research, technical, business, or other – to be
  treated as “Confidential Information”.

* **Business‑category rule** (`business_confidential_allowed`) explicitly states
  that a confidential item may be a business item (`conf_info.is_business`).

* **Hypothesis rule** (`only_technical_confidential_information`) requires that
  *whenever* an item is confidential, it must also be technical.

Because the model simultaneously permits a confidential item that is **business**
(`business_confidential_allowed`) and requires all confidential items to be
**technical** (`only_technical_confidential_information`), the two rules are
mutually inconsistent. A solver invoked with this module will therefore report
`unsat`, demonstrating that the hypothesis *“Confidential Information shall only
include technical information”* contradicts the definition supplied in the
Agreement.
